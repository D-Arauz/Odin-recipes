Hilachas


Description

Hilachas are a traditional Guatemalan Staple passed down by generations.This dish consists of shredded beef, potato and a red sauce. Takes about 3-4 hours to prepare and cook.


Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp garlic
  • 1 onion
  • 2 lb flank steak
  • 2 small pasilla Ancho chile (dry)
  • 1 California chile (dry)
  • 1 ground cumin
  • 8 tomatoes
  • 3 large tomatillos
  • 4 large white potatoes
  • 1 rice (optional)

Steps

  1. Cut the potatoes and onion in fourths
  2. Pan or Oven roast the chiles
  3. add all Ingredients in 2-3 quarts of water
  4. bring to a boil
  5. take out meat when it looks brittle and easy to shred
  6. cook rice
  7. liquify all Ingredients except potatoes
  8. add everything back to the pot with the sauce
  9. simmer for an extra 30 min til it thickens
  10. SERVE
