Fruit Smoothie


Description

A yogurt smoothie recipe that can be done with most if not all fruits and some vegetables.


Ingredients

  • one cup of Strawberries
  • one banana
  • half a cup of yogurt
  • one fourth cup of pineapple juice
  • one and a half spoons of white sugar
  • one tablespoon of orange juice
  • one teaspoon of milk
  • (optional)happiness

Steps

  1. combine all Ingredients and liquify
  2. SERVE
